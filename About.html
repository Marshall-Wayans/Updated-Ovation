About Us

Ovation Transportation is a Non-Emergency Transportation company that provides ambulatory, and
wheelchair transportation.

At Ovation Transportation, our goal is to ensure that our patients have access to services they need,
including reliable transportation. Our team of professioals work tirelessly to forge a personal
connection with each individual patient, making sure to coordinate an entire package tailores to your
specific need.

We are dedicated to getting you from Point A to Point B safely, on--time and with no hassle. Superior
services is our standard and what satisfies us is exceeding the expectations of our customers.

We put safety first, our driver training meets the highest standard required by law.

Each Driver Must:



  • Pass all required federal, state and local background checks.
  • Undergo a Department of Justice Background Check
  • Undergo an FBI Background Check
  • Undergo a National Sex Offenders Registry Screening
  • Pass employment drug and alcohol screening as allowed and required by law
